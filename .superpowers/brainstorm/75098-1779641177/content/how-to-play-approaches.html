How should "How to Play" work?

Three approaches — click to select your preference

A

One-time modal on first visit

A full-screen overlay appears the very first time someone opens the game. Three steps shown visually. Tap "Got it" to dismiss — never shown again (stored in localStorage). Fast to get into the game on repeat visits.

HOW TO PLAY ⚽
① Tap ball
to aim → ② Tap again
for power ↑ ③ Score streaks
to rank up 🔥
Got it!
B

Collapsible card above friend picker

A compact "How to play ▾" card sits permanently above the friend grid. Collapsed by default after first view. Users can re-open it anytime. Always accessible, never in the way.

How to play ⚽▾
① Tap the ball to freeze the direction bar
② Tap again to freeze the power bar
③ Score goals in a row to build your streak 🔥
④ Avoid the red zones — that's a miss
C

Contextual hints on the bars themselves

No separate instructions. Instead, the hint text already shown beneath each bar becomes more descriptive on the first round only — e.g. "Tap the ball to freeze the aim →" with a small animated arrow. After the first penalty, hints revert to the short versions.

DIRECTION ← →
👆 Tap the ball to lock your aim here →
POWER ↑
(appears after aim locked)
