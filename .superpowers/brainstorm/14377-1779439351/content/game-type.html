What kind of mini-game?

Pick the style that sounds most fun for the group

Penalty Shootout

Click a zone to shoot, the keeper dives randomly. Best streak / most goals on the leaderboard. Quick, arcade-y, very shareable.

⚽ 🦶

Keepy Uppy

Tap/click to keep the ball in the air — gravity drags it down. High score per friend tracked. Classic Google Doodle vibes.

🔮 📊

Score Predictor

Predict the score of upcoming matches before kick-off. Exact score = 3pts, correct result = 1pt. Separate predictor leaderboard.

🧠 ❓

Football Trivia

Daily World Cup trivia questions. First to answer correctly scores points. Separate trivia leaderboard alongside the sweepstake.
